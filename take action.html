Join Us!

It may seem like it's hard to make a difference when it's mostly huge corporations that are responsible for the damage caused to the environmental, but it's important that individuals take part too.


Things you can do include buying from sustainable companies, thrifting and choosing to buy clothing made of natural, better quality fabrics which will have a longer lifespan.


You do not have to completely stop buying fast fashion to help the cause, simply being aware and mindful when buying more is a good start. Together we can pressure governments to enforce regulations that would protect human rights and ensure that factory workers get a fair living wage.
