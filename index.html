170 million are engaged in child labour making textiles and garments
Around 8,000 synthetic chemicals are used in the fashion industry
Fashion industry contributes around 10% of all global greenhouse gas emissions
The fashion industry is responsible for 8% of carbon emissions
Worker rights of fast fashion employees are strongly violated
